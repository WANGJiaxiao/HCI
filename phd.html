Doctoral Program Advantages

Virtual University’s doctoral programs offer comprehensive academic support and resources to cultivate independent research capabilities and academic leadership.

Dual Mentorship System

Each doctoral student is guided by two advisors — one academic mentor and one industry mentor — providing comprehensive academic and career development support.

Full Scholarship Support

All doctoral students have the opportunity to receive full scholarships, including tuition waivers, living stipends, and research funding, ensuring they can focus fully on academic research.

Global Exchange Opportunities

We collaborate with top universities worldwide, offering opportunities to attend international conferences, participate in overseas exchange programs, and engage in joint training initiatives.

Industry-Academia Collaboration

Partnering with leading companies, we provide opportunities to translate research into real-world applications, enhancing both academic impact and societal value.

Academic Community Support

A vibrant academic community is fostered through regular seminars, workshops, and interdisciplinary exchanges that encourage collaboration and intellectual growth.

Career Development Guidance

We provide guidance for both academic and non-academic career paths, including academic job search, postdoctoral applications, and career planning in the industry.

Application Process and Requirements

We welcome outstanding talents from around the world to apply for doctoral programs at Virtual University. Below are the application process and basic requirements.

1

Identify Research Direction and Supervisor

Explore research fields in our schools, contact potential supervisors, discuss your research interests and program fit, and seek initial support.

2

Submit Online Application

Apply through our online application system and upload required materials, including a personal statement, research proposal, transcripts, and recommendation letters.

3

Academic Review and Interview

Your application will be reviewed by the school's admissions committee. Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview (online or in person).

4

Admission Notification

After passing the interview, you will receive an official admission offer within 4–6 weeks, including scholarship information and enrollment details.

5

Enrollment Registration

Confirm your acceptance, complete registration procedures, attend new student orientation, and begin your doctoral journey.

Academic Qualifications

  • A master’s degree (or expected to obtain one soon)
  • In some disciplines, exceptional bachelor's graduates may be admitted directly into the doctoral program
  • International degrees must be certified by the Chinese Service Center for Scholarly Exchange

Language Proficiency Requirements

  • Non-native English speakers must provide proof of English proficiency
  • IELTS: Minimum overall score of 6.5, with no sub-score below 6.0
  • TOEFL: Minimum total score of 90, with no section below 20
  • Some programs may require higher language proficiency

Required Documents

  • Online application form
  • Personal statement (including research interests and academic goals)
  • Detailed research proposal (typically 3–5 pages)
  • Transcripts (undergraduate and graduate studies)
  • At least two academic recommendation letters
  • Proof of English proficiency
  • Academic papers or research outcomes (if available)

Application Deadlines

  • Fall Intake: January 31 each year
  • Spring Intake: September 30 each year
  • Some programs have rolling admissions — please consult the specific school for details.

FAQ

Below are answers to common questions about applying for and pursuing a doctoral degree. For more information, please contact our admissions team.

The standard duration of the doctoral program at Virtual University is 4 years, including both coursework and dissertation research.

Students with relevant research experience or outstanding academic performance from their master's studies may apply to complete the program in 3 years.

The maximum period allowed for completion (including leaves of absence) is 6 years.

Our doctoral scholarships typically include the following components:

  • Monthly living stipend: RMB 5,000–8,000 per month (varies by discipline and research area)
  • Annual research funding: Approximately RMB 10,000
  • Conference support: Funding for participation in academic conferences
  • International exchange scholarships: Additional funding for outstanding applicants

Over 90% of our doctoral students receive some form of financial support. We are committed to ensuring that qualified students are not prevented from pursuing a Ph.D. due to financial constraints.

However, we recommend communicating with your primary supervisor of interest before submitting your application.

The admissions committee will match you with the most suitable supervisor based on your research interests and academic background.

If you are selected by multiple supervisors, you will have the opportunity to further discuss with them before finalizing your academic advisor.

Yes, we encourage doctoral students to participate in teaching activities.

This experience helps develop essential teaching skills and is particularly valuable for those considering an academic career.

Doctoral students often serve as teaching assistants for undergraduate or graduate courses, supporting professors with lectures, tutoring, and grading.

Outstanding students may also have the opportunity to lead course sessions independently and receive additional teaching compensation.

Graduates of our doctoral programs enjoy strong employment outcomes:

  • 40% join universities and research institutions for academic careers
  • 35% work in corporate departments in senior research roles
  • 15% enter government agencies or non-profit organizations in policy and consulting positions
  • 10% choose to start businesses or pursue postdoctoral training

In recent years, the employment rate of our doctoral graduates has remained consistently above 98%, with average starting salaries significantly higher than the industry average.
