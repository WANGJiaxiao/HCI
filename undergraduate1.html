Apply for Undergraduate Programs

To learn how to apply for undergraduate programs at outstanding universities, our admissions team will provide you with full support throughout the process.

Application Steps

1

Choose Program

Browse our undergraduate programs and choose the major that suits you best. Consider your interests, career goals and academic background.

2

Prepare Materials

Prepare application materials based on the selected program, including high school transcripts, recommendation letters, personal statements, etc.

3

Submit Application

Submit your application through our online application system and pay the application fee. Make sure all materials are submitted before the deadline.

4

Interview and Evaluation

Some majors may require interviews or additional tests. The admissions committee will evaluate your application materials and make an admission decision.

5

Admitted

After receiving the admission notice, complete the registration procedures as instructed, pay the tuition deposit and get ready for enrollment.

Application Timeline

Deadline

Deadline

Scholarship Application

Deadline

December 15th

All applicants will be automatically considered for merit-based scholarships. Specific scholarships may require additional application materials.

Application Requirements

Necessary Materials

High school transcript

Provide a complete high school transcript, including all courses and grades.

Recommendation letter

Usually, two recommendation letters from teachers are required. The recommenders should be familiar with your academic ability and personal qualities.

Personal statement

Write a personal statement elaborating on your academic interests, career goals, and why you chose our school.

Standardized test scores

Depending on the professional requirements, SAT, ACT or other standardized test scores may be needed.

Proof of English language proficiency

International students are required to provide TOEFL or IELTS scores to prove their English language proficiency.

Other Materials

Personal works

Art-related majors may require the submission of a portfolio to showcase your creative ability and artistic talent.

Interview

Some majors may require an interview to assess your communication skills and professional adaptability.

Extracurricular activity experience

Provide detailed information about participation in extracurricular activities, leadership roles, volunteer work or work experience.

Proof of special talent

If you have special talents in sports, music, art or other fields, please provide relevant proof materials.

Start the application

All application materials must be submitted before the deadline. Incomplete applications may affect the admission decision. We encourage applicants to submit their applications as early as possible to ensure there is sufficient time to handle any potential issues.

Q&A

For frequently asked questions about undergraduate applications and studies, please contact our admissions team if you have any other questions.

Undergraduate programs at outstanding universities usually last for four years. Some majors, such as engineering and architecture, may require 4.5 or 5 years. The acceleration program may allow students to complete their studies within three years.

International students can submit their applications through our online application system. In addition to the regular application materials, it is also necessary to provide proof of English language proficiency (TOEFL or IELTS scores), as well as a degree assessment report issued by the certification body. The application deadline for international students is December 1st.

Yes, the University of Excellence offers a variety of scholarships to undergraduate students, including academic scholarships, sports scholarships, art scholarships and scholarships for special talents, etc. Scholarship applications are usually made simultaneously with undergraduate applications. Some scholarships require additional application materials to be submitted. The deadline for scholarship applications is December 15th.

Yes, current students can apply for a major change, usually they need to meet the admission requirements of their target major. Students transferring from other universities need to submit their transcripts and course descriptions, and the school will assess the transferable credits. Transfer applications usually close before the start of each semester. For specific dates, please refer to the school's official website.
